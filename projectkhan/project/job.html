Job Search Portal

The main objective of the Project on Job Portal System is to manage the details of Employer, Employer Registration, Post Job, Search Job. It manages all the information about Employer, Search Job, Employee. The purpose of the project is to build an application program to reduce the manual work for managing the Employer, Employer Registration, Post Job , Interview. It tracks all the details about the Post Job, Interview, Interview, Search Job

The Solution of that problem is “Online job search Portal” where applicants easily find the jobs and employer can find suitable candidates for the job. Job Seeker logging into the system and he able to upload their information. Company representatives logging in may also access/search any information put up by Job Seeker.
